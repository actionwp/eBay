Description

Make efficient use of floor and air space with Action’s heavy-duty pallet rack. Heavy-duty uprights are ALL-WELDED 14 gauge steel and have a capacity of 16,000 lbs. Load Beams are heavy-duty, high tensile strength 14-gauge steel. Load Beams are 4” high x 96” long with a capacity of 5,000 lbs per level. Load Beams are adjustable every 2” on the upright. Built-in safety clip prevents detachment from the upright. Footplates are welded onto the column base to anchor unit to the floor. Unit comes complete with two uprights and four load beams. Action’s Tear-drop style pallet rack is interchangeable most tear-drop style manufacturers. Many other sizes available. Please e-mail amazon (at) actionwp.com with any questions.
